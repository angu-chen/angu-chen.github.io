Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on the decision now.

Look, I don’t really like tooting my own horn but for an assignment I guess I better start tooting. So there I was diligently working on my DevAcademy Foundations work. I am trying to catch up because for one reason or another I ended up starting the course late. My partner has gone to celebrate her friend finishing her PhD with some drinks after work. I get another text later on saying that she was going out for dinner as well. Perfect… More time for DevAcademy I thought. I am working and working and it hits 10.30pm and I receive another message. Can you pick me up? Now at this point, if you are expecting a crazy story filled with drama you are sadly wrong. I am pretty chilled and none of this really matters, however the ethnical dilemma came when I arrived and was asked if I wanted to come in as well because they had just set up the karaoke. First things first, I am tone deaf and can not sing for my life and second, its late and my brain is fried from a long day on the computer. What did I say? I said yes because I knew that celebrating a PhD properly is important and it is especially important to celebrate with friends. So one might ask was this an ethical decision? Well according to Google it is. Here is what the ai said.

“An ethical decision is a choice that considers the well-being of others and is guided by moral principles”

I considered the well being of the friend and the well being of the karaoke session and my judgement was guided by my moral principle that friends and friendships are incredibly important. I regret nothing and will do it again in a heartbeat. I sang Kelly Clarkson “Since you been gone” and “Where is the love?” by the Black eyed Peas.
