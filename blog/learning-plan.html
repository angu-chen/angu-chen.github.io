Career Pathway

I would like to become a well rounded software developer, using my expertise working in areas that I am passionate about and helps makes the world a better place. An example could be helping with technology for sustainability.

Strengths and Limitations

Honesty -I always try my best to speak the truth and present myself in a genuine way. Normally what you see is what you get and part of that is I also always try to be honest with myself. This may take the form of acknowledging how I am feeling etc.

Integrity - I take pride in my work but there is a caveat, I try my best to not let my ego get involved. This manifest itself as not taking constructive comments personally and not being stuck to my ideas etc.

Love of Learning - I love learning but my relationship with it is sometimes a bit conflicting. I say this because the flip side is sometimes I get quite frustrated not knowing, but I guess you could argue not knowing allows for space to learn. All very philosophical, but the point is, I secretly love the challenge and the process of getting better at what I am doing.

Team PlayerI like to think of myself as a true team player. I prefer working in a team. Not only do I get more done but I also find it more motivating when I am depended on. I love the ability to talk over things and building relationships with teammates.

Limitations - Sometimes I get overwhelmed. Overwhelmed by the sheer size of a project or but the copious amounts of information available. Another limitation for me is the ability to stay focused for long periods of time. Normally I work in 1 hour intervals with 5 minute breaks. This is normally quite flexible based on what level of involvement I am in with the task. However I find I am always more focused after a 10minute walk or a bit of sunshine.

What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to improve my “Think like a programmer” mentality and be able to break large tasks down to more manageable chunks.

I would like to improve my ability to stay focus on the task at hand

I would like to further improve knowing when I need a small break in order to be more on task

Get better at establishing and maintaining good work environments

My Commitment to how I will manage my workload in this programme.

I will treat everyone with respect and in a courteous manner. This means respecting them both as a co-worker as well a person. I will practice active listening first and foremost. I think this is a great way to approach working in a team. I will try to stay honest with what is possible and insure that I take breaks even when the workload is high.

When things are building up and are starting to get to me, I will let the facilitators and the appropriate people know how I am feeling. I will ask for advice and continue to do what I can. I will also acknowledge that the work is cyclic and that it will pass if I continue to chip away. I will continue to take small breaks and do exercise so my well-being to maintained.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

I will seek help from the facilitation team, and my support network when I need help. For technical help, I will follow the guidelines given during Foundations by DevAcademy and for emotional support I will seek it through my support network i.e. family and friends. However if it gets to this, I will ensure that I let the facilitation team know as well as the appropriate individuals.

A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

I expect the facilitation team to treat me and the rest of the cohort with empathy and respect regarding our questions and needs. I understand that this may not be possible all the time or mistakes might happen.

Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things.

I have no kids however I have a part-time job. I am juggling 20 hour work week while completing this course. Combine this commitment with essential exercise to maintain my well-being means I may not be able to be present 100% outside of the allocated working times. However I am extremely flexible and engaged.
